If you end up at what seems to be a new account when you log into the Gumballs & Dragons, you need not to worry! The game has been transferred to a new publisher, but you can easily transfer your previous progress with a transfer code. Enter your transfer code under "settings" and hit confirm, and you will be able to continue from where you where before!

How do I get my transfer code?

If you still have the old app installed, simply open it and the transfer code will be shown for you. You can also:

1. If you logged into the game using your Google account we've sent the transfer code to you via e-mail. So check your inbox for a message from us.

2. If you didn't use a Google account, you there's no need to worry. Just tap "Contact customer support" and we will help you verify your account and send you a transfer code.

To get the transfer code faster, please submit the following information when contacting us to make it easier for us to verify your identity:

1. Tell us your in-game nickname

2. We're sure you've used an account from either Facebook or Google to log onto the game. Tell us your account and we will be able to find you in our system.

3. Have you made any in-app purchases before? If yes, please submit a receipt from one of these. They are mailed to your inbox at each purchase.

4. Please tell us your level, how many gumballs you have (give or take), how many airships you have, when you last logged into the game, and how many gems you have left.
